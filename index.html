Our story

Why El Paso choose us

Next Level Barber Studio isn't your average shop—we're a collective of nine independent master barbers, each bringing their own signature style and loyal following to one premium location on Pebble Hills. From classic cuts to modern fades, straight razor shaves to intricate braids, every barber in our studio operates at the top of their game.

Our space is spotless, modern, and designed for the full barbershop experience: conversation, craftsmanship, and confidence when you walk out the door. Book your favorite barber directly or walk in—either way, you're getting next-level work in East El Paso's most respected chair.
